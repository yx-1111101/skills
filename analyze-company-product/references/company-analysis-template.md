# Company Analysis Template

Use when the user wants a fuller company or financing-oriented memo rather than a narrow feature teardown.

## Required Sections

- Team and organization:
  Founders, key leaders, notable backgrounds, and visible org capability. If structure is not public, provide a cautious inference.
- Product philosophy and positioning:
  What the company believes about the product or market, who it serves, and how it positions itself.
- Financing:
  Rounds, investors, dates, disclosed amounts, and the signal carried by the financing pattern.
- Commercial model and product matrix:
  Revenue model, pricing motion, customer tiers, and product lineup.
- Technical advantage:
  Technology, infrastructure, workflow, data, integration, supply, brand, or execution edge. Distinguish core capability from packaging.
- Final judgment:
  Main strengths, funding logic, transferable lessons, and objective forward view.

## Judgment Prompts

- Why did investors believe this company mattered at this moment?
- Is the advantage driven by timing, execution, market heat, or genuine capability?
- Which strengths look durable, and which are likely to compress?
- What lessons are portable without copying the whole product?
- Which future scenarios appear most plausible: breakout growth, strong niche, acquisition, or commoditization?
